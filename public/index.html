App Capabilities

Designed to support every physical assessment component with a high-fidelity, streamlined interface.

🏃‍♂️

Comprehensive Cardio

Scores are auto-populated based on user input for the 2-Mile Run, 20-Meter HAMR Shuttle Run, or the 2.0-Kilometer Walk standards. All data is kept locally on your device.

💪

Strength & Core Grading

Support for standard push-ups, hand-release push-ups, sit-ups, cross-leg crunches, and forearm planks. Calculates point boundaries instantly based on your gender and age group.

📏

Waist-to-Height Ratio

Evaluate body composition risk categories. Input waist circumference and height to get points awarded and clear low, moderate, or high risk status mappings.

🎯

Goal Targets ("What Do I Need?")

Choose a target composite tier (Pass: 75.0, Good: 80.0, Excellent: 90.0, Max: 100.0) and see exactly what performance metrics you need in remaining events, relative to your current WHtR.

🛡️

100% Privacy & Offline-First

Zero external analytics. Your fitness scores are highly sensitive; thus, all calculations are made locally, and score history resides securely on your device's sandbox storage.

📊

Local Progress Logs

Save, track, and export assessment records. Maintain a complete chronological log of your fitness progression, ready to import/export if you change devices.

Featured Interactive Tools

Interactive HAMR Beep Player & Run Tracker

Train realistically. The app includes a fully functional 20m HAMR shuttle run audio beep player, dynamically announcing shuttle level changes. For runners, the built-in stopwatch track automatically inputs run durations.
