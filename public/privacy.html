Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Air Force PFRA Calculator app (the "Application") handles your personal data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe and secure user experience.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed to operate completely offline. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, physical performance metrics, or health-related data. All calculation inputs, including:

  • Gender
  • Age group
  • Waist-to-Height Ratio (WHtR) measurements
  • Fitness event scores (run times, HAMR shuttles, push-up/sit-up repetitions, plank durations)

are processed strictly locally on your device. Once calculations are completed, data is stored only in your device's local storage if you explicitly choose to save your score history. This history never leaves your device.

2. Third-Party Services and Permissions

The Application does not require registration, user accounts, or internet connectivity to perform calculations. We do not integrate third-party trackers, analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or cloud databases that could collect your usage details.

3. Camera and Storage Permissions

No special device permissions (such as location, camera, contacts, or microphone) are requested or required to operate this Application.

4. Data Retention

Because we do not collect any data, we do not store your data on external servers. The history data saved within the Application resides solely in your device's secure sandbox and will be permanently deleted if you uninstall the Application or clear its storage.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
