Core Features

🛡️
100% Local Privacy
All calculations and score history are stored strictly on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your fitness data.
🏃
Cardio Assessment
Calculate scores for the 2-Mile Run, 20m HAMR Shuttle Run, or the 2.0km Walk (Pass/Fail). Includes a built-in HAMR beep player!
💪
Strength & Core
Supports standard push-ups, hand-release push-ups, sit-ups, crunches, and forearm planks with real-time score grading.
📏
Waist-to-Height Ratio
Quickly evaluate your WHtR score with visual risk indicators, completely aligned with the new uniform scoring brackets.
🎯
Goal Targets ("What do I need?")
Specify your target tier (Pass, Good, Excellent, Max) and instantly see what physical scores you need to achieve it.
📊
Progress Logging
Save history logs locally to track your fitness progression over time and check passing trends.
